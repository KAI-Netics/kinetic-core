### Post-Part 108 / Rural Eastern Shore / Full CURN Carrier License Track

This is the Tier 3 expansion that transforms KAI-Netics from a Baltimore metro operator
into a statewide drone infrastructure platform. Three gates must open before this track activates:

**Gate 1: FAA Part 108 Final Rule Publication**
- Current status: Rulemaking in progress. Timeline uncertain but expected 2026-2027.
- Action: Monitor FAA NPRM docket. James L. Scott, Esq. tracks and alerts.
- Impact: Unlocks BVLOS operations without per-flight waivers, enabling economical rural routes.
- KAI-Netics prep: Accumulate Part 107 POC flight data now — this data strengthens Part 108 applications.

**Gate 2: CURN Full Carrier License Application**
- What it is: A formal commercial operator license to operate on CURN corridors statewide,
  not just at the Baltimore endpoint. Issued by UROC/UMD under FAA coordination.
- Prerequisites: Established UROC relationship (via internship), demonstrated POC operations,
  BVLOS waiver or Part 108 approval, MONK trust layer as compliance infrastructure.
- Action: Build the UROC relationship NOW through internship. Position Kevin as the first
  commercial operator UROC wants to issue a full carrier license to.
- Timeline target: Year 2 Q1 (January 2027) — file after CAKO Cohort 1 graduates.

**Gate 3: Rural Eastern Shore Anchor Customer**
- Target customers: Eastern Shore Community Health Centers (CHCs), rural UMMS affiliates,
  Shore Regional Health (University of Maryland), Peninsula Regional Medical Center (TidalHealth)
- Why Eastern Shore: Low population density + poor last-mile infrastructure + long truck routes =
  maximum GHG displacement argument + maximum Jevons demand expansion opportunity.
- Action: Identify one rural health clinic contact by Month 9. Initial outreach by Month 12.
- MONK angle: Rural operators have no compliance infrastructure — MONK licensing is the entry point.

**Rural Eastern Shore Expansion Roadmap (Year 2):**

| Milestone | Target | Gate Required |
|-----------|--------|---------------|
| CURN full carrier license filed | Q1 2027 | Gates 1+2 |

**DaI Franchise Model (Year 2+):**
The end state: EAF trains operators nationally under CAKO, FP licenses the DFR pad
infrastructure model to other operators, and MONK licenses the AI orchestration stack
as a SaaS product. This is the Tesla/Supercharger model at full deployment — KAI-Netics
does not need to operate every pad to earn revenue from every pad.
