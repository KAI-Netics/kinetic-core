### TIER 1 USE CASES — Market Analysis

#### 1. Medical Supply / Surgical Kit Delivery
- **Market size:** US medical supply last-mile delivery $8B+/year; MD SAM = Baltimore metro health systems
- **Anchor customer:** UMMS LOC (Sparrows Point → 11 hospitals); Pat Vizzard, VP Supply Chain
- **Kevin's edge:** Medline insider — knows surgical kit delivery pain, cost-per-delivery benchmarks
- **Revenue model:** Per-delivery fee ($15–$50/delivery depending on SLA) + managed service contract
- **Competitive moat:** MWVOB + green collar workforce (CAKO) + MONK trust layer
- **MEIA story:** Replace diesel truck trips; quantifiable GHG reduction per lane

#### 2. Lab Specimen / Blood Sample Transport
- **Market size:** Clinical lab logistics ~$5B US; high-frequency, time-critical, sub-5lb
- **Anchor customers:** Johns Hopkins (Dr. Amukele connection), UMMS lab network, LabCorp, Quest
- **History:** UROC proved feasibility — first drone delivery of live kidney, 2019, Baltimore
- **Revenue model:** Per-delivery SLA contract with clinical penalty clauses
- **Kevin's edge:** UMGC student + Dr. Amukele academic connection = warm introduction
- **MEIA story:** Clinical efficiency + emissions; life-saving speed improvement

#### 3. Prescription / Pharmacy Delivery
- **Market size:** US retail pharmacy delivery $3B+; growing rapidly post-COVID
- **Anchor customers:** CVS, Walgreens, independent Baltimore pharmacies, UMMS outpatient pharmacy
- **Target zones:** Baltimore City food/pharmacy deserts (Sandtown, Cherry Hill, Park Heights)
- **Revenue model:** Per-delivery subscription + pharmacy partnership contract
- **MEIA story:** Health equity — pharmacy deserts mirror food deserts in Baltimore's most
  vulnerable neighborhoods. Drone delivery = access equity + emissions reduction.
- **Kevin's edge:** UMGC student status opens academic conversations about health equity research

#### 4. Legal Document Courier
- **Market size:** Same-day legal courier Baltimore metro ~$50M/year (est.)
- **Customers:** Law firms (Inner Harbor, downtown), courthouses, title companies, settlement firms
- **Revenue model:** Per-delivery ($20–$75 same-hour premium) + law firm retainer
- **Kevin's edge:** No specific insider knowledge needed — Part 107 + reliability is the pitch
- **MEIA story:** Vehicle trip elimination (simple, calculable)

#### 5. Emergency Supply to Resiliency Hubs
- **Market:** Baltimore City Resiliency Hub network (7+ hubs); MEA Resilient Maryland Program
- **Customers:** Groundswell, Living Classrooms, Baltimore Office of Sustainability, BCHD
- **Revenue model:** Grant-funded SLA + per-event emergency delivery contract
- **Kevin's edge:** EAF/CAKO green collar workforce is the mission; MWVOB + 501c3 alignment
- **MEIA story:** Direct climate resilience infrastructure — the most compelling MEIA use case

#### 6. Government / Municipal Small Supply
- **Market:** Baltimore City agencies, MD state offices, county government facilities
- **Revenue model:** Government service contract (GSA-style), recurring
- **Kevin's edge:** CISSP + PMP = government procurement credibility; MBE = set-aside eligibility
- **MEIA story:** Green government procurement mandate; emissions reduction for public fleet
