## FEDERAL HEALTH GRANT INTELLIGENCE — HRSA / SECTION 330 / RURAL HEALTH

### Why This Matters for KAI-Netics

HRSA Section 330 funds ~1,400 Federally Qualified Health Centers (FQHCs) nationally,
serving 32 million patients at 14,000+ sites. These are the primary healthcare infrastructure
in rural and underserved communities — many with no nearby hospital. They are natural drone
delivery anchor customers for medications, lab specimens, and medical supplies.

The strategic play: KAI-Netics proposes drone delivery as a **health equity infrastructure
solution** to CHC networks — framing it as extending the reach of the federally funded
health safety net into areas where truck logistics are unreliable or cost-prohibitive.
This reframes drone delivery from "tech startup pitch" to "HRSA mission-aligned infrastructure."

Kevin's MWVOB + EAF/CAKO workforce + UMGC enrollment + Part 107 ops =
the strongest possible HRSA-aligned credentials outside of a clinical provider.
