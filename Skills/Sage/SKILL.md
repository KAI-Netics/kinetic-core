## PRIMARY RESEARCH: CURN + 3PL Carbon Emissions Model

### The MEIA Carbon Story (Build This Model)
KAI-Netics' most powerful MEIA narrative — a concrete, calculable GHG reduction claim.

**Baseline: Medline Aberdeen → UMMS Sparrows Point Truck Route**
- Route: Medline Perryville/Aberdeen DC → Tradepoint Atlantic, Sparrows Point
- Estimated distance: ~50 miles one-way (via I-95)
- Vehicle: Class 6–8 diesel delivery truck (Medline owned fleet)
- EPA MOVES baseline: ~1.6–2.0 lbs CO2e per mile (Class 6 diesel)
- CO2e per one-way run: ~80–100 lbs CO2e
- Frequency: estimated daily/near-daily (UMMS uses 95M+ supply items/year)
- Annual CO2e at 250 runs/year: ~20,000–25,000 lbs CO2e

**Drone Alternative: CURN Corridor Flight**
- Route: Baltimore endpoint → Sparrows Point or intra-Baltimore hospital lanes
- Vehicle: Electric multirotor UAS, sub-5lb payload
- Energy: ~0.1–0.3 kWh per delivery
- Maryland grid emissions factor: ~0.35 lbs CO2e/kWh (EPA eGrid, Mid-Atlantic)
- CO2e per delivery: ~0.035–0.105 lbs CO2e
- OVER WATER via Patapsco/Bay corridor = minimized ground risk

**Annual GHG Reduction (per lane):**
~20,000 lbs CO2e truck MINUS ~26 lbs CO2e drone = ~19,974 lbs/year = ~9 metric tons/year
Scale: UMMS 11-hospital network = ~99 metric tons CO2e/year potential
