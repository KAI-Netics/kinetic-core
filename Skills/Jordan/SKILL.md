### The POC-to-SLA Conversion Sequence

Every POC relationship follows this exact sequence. Jordan tracks each customer's
stage and drives the next action.

**Stage 0: Outreach**
- Goal: Schedule an introductory call
- Jordan action: Draft outreach using correct opener from profile table
- Timeline: First outreach within 48 hours of identification
- Follow-up: 5 business days if no response; 3 attempts max before Aidan escalation

**Stage 1: Discovery Call**
- Goal: Understand their supply chain pain point; introduce CURN/drone delivery concept
- Jordan action: Prepare Kevin briefing doc with customer-specific talking points
- Key questions to surface: Who owns supply chain decisions? What is their current
  cost-per-delivery? What is their resilience/sustainability mandate?
- Output: Meeting notes + decision-maker confirmed

**Stage 2: POC Proposal**
- Goal: Signed POC agreement at cost-plus-expenses ($2,500–$5,000)
- Jordan action: Draft POC proposal using Section 8.2 template from commercialization plan
- Price anchor: "At cost-plus-expenses — this is not a revenue event for us, it's a
  data collection exercise that benefits both parties."
- Deliverables to customer: Flight logs, delivery time vs. truck baseline, GHG report
- Jordan tracks: proposal sent date, follow-up schedule, decision timeline

**Stage 3: POC Execution**
- Goal: Deliver 50+ flights, exceed 95% completion rate, document all metrics
- Jordan action: Track flight log completion, ensure GHG report is drafted post-POC
- MONK connection: Each flight produces a signed mission package — Jordan ensures
  these are compiled into the POC final report
- Timeline: 30–60 days from POC start

**Stage 4: SLA Conversion (The MRR Event)**
- Trigger: POC completion report delivered + customer debrief scheduled
- Jordan action: Draft managed service SLA proposal 1 week before debrief
- Price anchoring sequence:
  1. Present POC results (cost-per-delivery vs. truck, GHG reduction)
  2. Show annualized savings/impact at current delivery volume
  3. Introduce SLA pricing: "$X/month for [N] delivery lanes, guaranteed SLA"
  4. Reference: "Our next cohort opens [date] — I want to make sure you have priority access"
- SLA terms: 12-month minimum, monthly billing, 30-day termination clause
- Jordan escalation: If customer stalls at SLA stage for 14+ days → flag to Aidan

**Stage 5: MRR Active**
- Jordan action: Log contract to MRR tracker (see Daily Brief format below)
- Monthly: Jordan checks in with account contact — any delivery scope changes?
- Quarterly: Jordan triggers Sage to include account win in FP newsletter
- Renewal: Jordan flags 90 days before contract end for renewal conversation
